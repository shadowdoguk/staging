About the Studio

Founded in Lisbon, Studio is an interior design practice working across Portugal and Europe. We believe that great design starts with listening — to how you live, how you move through space, and how you want to feel at home.

Every project is a collaboration. We bring rigour to the process and warmth to the outcome, balancing aesthetic precision with the lived-in reality of everyday life.

Our work spans private residences, boutique hospitality projects, and carefully considered commercial spaces.
